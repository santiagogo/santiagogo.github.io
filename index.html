About me

Im a Colombian internet entrepreneur. I like design, lighting, photography and am a very visual person. I have a passion for history and love cooking and eating good food. I'm easy going, open minded and most people consider me a liberal.

I spend more time on the internet than I probably should and professionally have always ended up in internet related ventures. I consider the internet one of the most amazing inventions in the history of humankind and by far the most important one in my lifetime. Why such a big fan? because I am absolutely convinced that the internet is the ultimate catalyzer for better education, communication, globalization and political change. This will make our lives more fullfilling, reduce inequality & poverty, and ultimately build a better world.

I'm also a sucker for fast cars, domain names and japanese things.
